3D Interacting Hand Pose Estimation by Hand De-occlusion and Removal
Hao Meng*, Sheng Jin*, Wentao Liu, Chen Qian, Mengxiang Lin, Wanli Ouyang, Ping Luo
(*=equal contribution)
ECCV, 2022
project (dataset) / paper / arxiv / code

Interacting Hand pose estimation is very challenging, due to (1) severe hand-hand occlusion and (2) ambiguity caused by the homogeneous appearance of hands. In this work, we propose a novel Hand De-occlusion and Removal (HDR) framework to perform hand de-occlusion and distractor removal. We also propose the first large-scale synthetic amodal hand dataset, termed Amodal InterHand Dataset (AIH), to facilitate model training and promote the development of the related research.
